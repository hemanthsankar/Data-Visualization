Age Distribution Histogram

This histogram displays the distribution of client ages in the dataset. It provides insights into the age demographics of the clients. The x-axis represents age, and the y-axis represents the count of clients. The histogram is created with 20 bins to show the distribution more granularly. The presence of a kernel density estimate (KDE) plot helps visualize the probability density of age values. The visualization is set on a white grid background and is color-coded using the "Set2" palette. A title and labels provide context and information. The resulting graph is saved as "graph1.png."
